The one connector that matters most

Most competing designs would draw Procurement and Supply Chain as two dashboards that happen to share a chat box. Here they're wired together directly: the Procurement Agent's OEM recommendation calls into the Supply Chain Agent's material-risk lookup, and a vehicle's confidence score is penalized (up to 20 points) when its recommended battery cell chemistry traces back to a high-risk supplier base — visible on every readiness score, not buried on a separate tab.
